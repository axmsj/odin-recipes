Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Flat Noodles
  • Meat Sauce
  • Ricotta

Steps needed to make Lasagna:

  1. Spread a thin layer of pasta sauce int he bottom of a baking dish.
  2. Make a layer of cooked lasagna noodles.
  3. Spread an even later of the ricotta cheese.
  4. Spread an even later of meat sauce.
  5. Repeat those layers two times.
  6. Top it with a final later of noodles, sauce, mozzarella, and parmesan cheese.
