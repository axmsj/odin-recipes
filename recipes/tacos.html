Tacos Recipe

Ingredients

  • Tortilla or Shell
  • Meat (Beef)
  • Vegetables
  • Sauce
  • Cheese Salsa
  • Guacamole

Steps to make Tacos:

  1. The shell. The first step to building the ultimate taco is selecting the shell. Soft or Hard.
  2. Protein: Chicken, Pork, Beef, or Shrimp.
  3. Cheese: Adding the cheese can be a crucial to a good taco.
  4. Add your toppings: Sour Cream, Guacamole, Beans.
  5. Do not forget the veggies.
