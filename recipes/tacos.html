Steak Tacos

Description

Steak tacos are a flavorful dish featuring tender, marinated grilled beef served on warm tortillas. Topped with fresh onions, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime, they offer a delicious blend of savory and zesty flavors, making them perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Avocado
  • Onion
  • Lime
  • Cilantro
  • Cheese
  • Tortillas

Steps

  1. Grill the Steak
  2. Prepare the Tortillas
  3. Mash Avocado
  4. Sprinkle Cheese
  5. Top with Onion and Cilantro
  6. Squeeze Fresh Lime
  7. Serve
